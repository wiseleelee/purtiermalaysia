Download & File Help

ZIP Files: You need WINZIP, 7zip or any equivalent program to unzip the files into your hard drive. If you don't have any ZIP extractor programs, you can download it at 7zip.org and install into your computer.
PDF Files - you can view PDF files in Adobe Acrobat Reader. If this program is not available in your computer, you can download a free program at: https://get.adobe.com/reader/
